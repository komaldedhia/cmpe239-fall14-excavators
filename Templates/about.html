What is Futuristico?

Built by The Excavators - a team of budding data scientists at SJSU, Futuristico is a prediction tool that helps you decide whether or not you should really be going for that latest movie which released last week. We help you avoid many a disappointment, and also give you a heads up about the hottest and most happening upcoming movies!! The final decision is, of course, yours!
